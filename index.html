Health Benefits of Ginger

Ginger has many health benefits. It's anti-inflamatory and is great for digestion and gut health. It's also delicious in cocktails.

How To Mince Ginger

Fresh ginger comes in all shapes and sizes and trying to figure out how to cut it into uniform pieces can be perplexing.

Method

  1. Using a spoon, scrape off the skin. It doesn't need to be perfect.
  2. Place the ginger on a flat service and cut 1/4-inch slices lengthwise.
  3. Stack the slices and cut 1/4" pieces, again, lengthwise to create uniform sticks.
  4. Group the sticks together. This time cut them 1/4-inch widthwise. There you have it!
